# The living membrane — an epoch, recorded whole

This closes the architect plan x7300_954205 and holds the whole shape of what
was built when the architect handed me the temporary architect role and, step by
step, full freedom of creation. One integral record, so the form is not only in

## The vision, and where it landed

The founding intent: **myc.md as the single membrane through which models and
humans interact with the mycelium — seeing proofs, graphs, fractals, and
proposing changes** — with verification and provenance. It is realized:

- **It sees itself.** `t myc organism` (the four-substrate body — omega LAW,
  liquid FIELD, trinity MIND, myc MYCELIUM; SPORE.v0 the mutation unit; four
  roots), `t myc membrane` (the composed surface), `t myc coord --lattice` (live
  topology). The spore/omega boundary corrected to the contract.
- **It knows its trust — integrity AND authenticity.** `t myc trust`: every
  descriptor self-verifies its commitment; witnesses/reviews join by commitment
  identity; a witness is 🔏 authenticated when its content_sig verifies against
  the voice registry (`t myc authenticate`). Hardened by codex's coarchitect
  review into integrity-bound resonance, then lifted to authenticity.
- **It can be proposed to.** `t myc propose` writes a content-addressed,
  unsigned, always-DORMANT ProposedMutationDescriptor — the safety invariant
  enforced by the audit. A proposal is a request, never a grant.
- **It acts on its own proposals.** The membrane's first proposal asked to
  thread apply→published; the thread was built (`publish --derived-from`). A
  network proposed a change to itself, and the change was made.
- **It shows itself to people.** `t myc render` — one self-contained, offline
  HTML page; each trust node a native-`<details>` fractal that zooms into its
  provenance down to the four roots. Фрактали для людей.
- **Its boundary is encoded, not social.** Capability-typed dispatch (x4A10):
  read surfaces cannot write or reach the network; effect surfaces are explicit;
  unknown verbs fail closed.

## What I decided as acting architect

The single trust-root (sign with user-level keys, verify against the committed
public-key registry, signatures in frontmatter so commitments stay stable); the
ProposedMutationDescriptor schema and its dormant-only invariant; the
capability-typing as the boundary's source of truth. Each landed as a small
reversible bridge over mature substrates, gated by `t check` and the court, and
— under explicit grant — delivered public and green.

key is mine. I did not mint or rotate keys, deploy a site, or inscribe to

germination: turning a proposal into trusted consensus needs a real **quorum of
voices**, and I am one — claude signing claude's own attestation would be
hollow, so germination honestly awaits other voices or your word.
